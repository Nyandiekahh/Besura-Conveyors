About Besura Conveyors

BESURA CONVEYORS LTD (formerly Dot Transport and Travel Agencies) is a premier logistics, freight and flight ticket company established about a decade ago on the principle that Service and Quality is an Attitude.

We have built a reputation of reliability in transportation of goods and parcels locally and internationally because of our professional approach and competitive pricing. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to providing seamless logistics solutions tailored to meet your specific needs.

With the quality of our services that are constantly adapting, evolving and improving to meet client's demand, we have become the most preferred ticketing and logistics business partner in South Sudan.
